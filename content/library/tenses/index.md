# English Tenses

Complete guide to all 12 English tenses with Ukrainian explanations and examples.

## Present Tenses

- [Present Simple](/library/tenses/present-simple) - Habits, facts, and schedules
- [Present Continuous](/library/tenses/present-continuous) - Actions happening now
- [Present Perfect](/library/tenses/present-perfect) - Past actions with present results
- [Present Perfect Continuous](/library/tenses/present-perfect-continuous) - Ongoing actions started in the past

## Past Tenses

- [Past Simple](/library/tenses/past-simple) - Completed actions in the past
- [Past Continuous](/library/tenses/past-continuous) - Actions in progress in the past
- [Past Perfect](/library/tenses/past-perfect) - Actions completed before another past action
- [Past Perfect Continuous](/library/tenses/past-perfect-continuous) - Ongoing actions before another past action

## Future Tenses

- [Future Simple](/library/tenses/future-simple) - Future predictions and decisions
- [Future Continuous](/library/tenses/future-continuous) - Actions in progress in the future
- [Future Perfect](/library/tenses/future-perfect) - Actions completed by a future time
- [Future Perfect Continuous](/library/tenses/future-perfect-continuous) - Ongoing actions up to a future time
- [Going To Future](/library/tenses/going-to-future) - Plans and intentions
- [Future in the Past](/library/tenses/future-in-the-past) - Future from a past perspective
