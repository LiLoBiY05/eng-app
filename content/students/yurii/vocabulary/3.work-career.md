# Work & Career Vocabulary

Words and phrases used in professional contexts — job applications, workplace, and career development.

## Job Application Phrases

| # | Word/Phrase | Part of Speech | Ukrainian | Definition | Example |
|---|-------------|----------------|-----------|------------|---------|
| 1 | **covering letter** | Noun | Супровідний лист | A letter sent with a job application | Always include a covering letter with your CV. |
| 2 | **gain work experience** | Verb phrase | Набути досвіду роботи | To get practical knowledge through working | It's important to gain work experience before graduating. |
| 3 | **in response to** | Preposition phrase | У відповідь на | As a reaction to something | In response to the feedback, we updated the course. |
| 4 | **part-time job** | Noun phrase | Робота на неповний робочий день | Employment for fewer hours than a full-time position | She has a part-time job at a café while studying. |
| 5 | **practical experience of** | Noun phrase | Практичний досвід у | Real-world knowledge gained through doing | Employers value practical experience of working in a team. |
| 6 | **prove ability to** | Verb phrase | Довести здатність до | To demonstrate that you can do something | You need to prove your ability to work under pressure. |
| 7 | **pursue a career** | Verb phrase | Будувати кар'єру | To actively work towards a professional goal | She wants to pursue a career in education. |
| 8 | **summer job** | Noun phrase | Літня підробітка | Temporary employment during summer holidays | He got a summer job at a local restaurant. |
| 9 | **valuable** | Adjective | Цінний, важливий | Worth a lot; very useful or important | This experience will be very valuable for your career. |
| 10 | **volunteer** | Verb / Noun | Волонтерити; волонтер | To offer to do something without being paid | She volunteered at the local library during the summer. |
| 11 | **welcome an opportunity** | Verb phrase | Вітати можливість | To be happy about a chance to do something | I welcome the opportunity to work with such a talented team. |
| 12 | **well suited** | Adjective phrase | Добре підходить, відповідає | Appropriate or right for a particular purpose | Her skills make her well suited for this role. |
| 13 | **work placement** | Noun phrase | Виробнича практика | A period of work experience as part of a course | The course includes a six-week work placement. |

## Workplace Skills & Actions

| # | Word/Phrase | Part of Speech | Ukrainian | Definition | Example |
|---|-------------|----------------|-----------|------------|---------|
| 14 | **dedicated member of** | Noun phrase | Відданий/старанний член (команди) | A committed and loyal participant in a group | She's a dedicated member of the student council. |
| 15 | **excellent command of** | Noun phrase | Відмінне володіння (мовою) | A very good ability to use a language | He has an excellent command of written English. |
| 16 | **lead a team** | Verb phrase | Керувати командою | To be in charge of and guide a group of people | She was asked to lead a team of five researchers. |
| 17 | **meet deadlines** | Verb phrase | Виконувати роботу вчасно | To complete work by the required time | It's essential to meet deadlines in any professional job. |
| 18 | **well-developed** | Adjective | Добре розвинений | Fully or thoroughly developed | She has well-developed communication skills. |
| 19 | **work alongside** | Verb phrase | Працювати поряд/разом | To work with someone as a colleague | I had the chance to work alongside experienced professionals. |
| 20 | **work in the field of** | Verb phrase | Працювати у сфері | To be employed in a particular area or discipline | He works in the field of medical research. |
| 21 | **put into** | Phrasal verb | Вкладати (зусилля, гроші, час) | To invest time, money, or effort into something | He put a lot of effort into his final project. |
| 22 | **pursuing** | Verb (present participle) | Прагнути, займатися чимось | Actively working to achieve a goal | She's pursuing a career in medicine. |
| 23 | **contribute to** | Verb phrase | Робити внесок у, сприяти | To help cause or produce a result | Regular reading contributes to better writing skills. |
| 24 | **considerable** | Adjective | Значний, чималий | Large in size, amount, or degree | She has made considerable progress this term. |
