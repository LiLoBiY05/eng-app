# Education Vocabulary

Words and phrases related to education, studying, and academic life.

## Academic Actions & Verb Phrases

| # | Word/Phrase | Part of Speech | Ukrainian | Definition | Example |
|---|-------------|----------------|-----------|------------|---------|
| 1 | **fall behind** | Phrasal verb | Відставати (у навчанні) | To make slower progress than others; to fail to keep up | She fell behind in her studies after missing several classes. |
| 2 | **get a degree** | Verb phrase | Отримати ступінь/диплом | To complete a university course and receive a qualification | He worked hard to get a degree in computer science. |
| 3 | **get a scholarship** | Verb phrase | Отримати стипендію | To be awarded money to pay for education | She got a scholarship to study at Oxford University. |
| 4 | **get expelled** | Verb phrase | Бути відрахованим (зі школи) | To be officially removed from a school as a punishment | He got expelled from school for cheating on the exam. |
| 5 | **get top marks** | Verb phrase | Отримувати найвищі оцінки | To achieve the highest possible grades | She always gets top marks in mathematics. |
| 6 | **major in** | Verb phrase | Спеціалізуватися з (предмету) | To study something as the main subject at university | She decided to major in psychology at university. |
| 7 | **scrape through** | Phrasal verb | Ледве пройти/скласти | To barely pass an exam or get through a difficult situation | He scraped through his final exams with minimum grades. |
| 8 | **settle down** | Phrasal verb | Заспокоїтися, зосередитися | To become calm and focused; to start concentrating | Please settle down — the lesson is about to begin. |
| 9 | **write a dissertation** | Verb phrase | Писати дисертацію | To produce a long research paper for a university degree | She spent two years writing her dissertation on climate change. |
| 10 | **acquire a skill** | Verb phrase | Набути навичку | To learn or develop an ability through practice | It takes time to acquire a skill like public speaking. |
| 11 | **memorise** | Verb | Запам'ятовувати, вивчати напам'ять | To learn something so that you can remember it exactly | We used to memorise long poems at school. |
| 12 | **supervise** | Verb | Наглядати, керувати | To watch over and direct someone's work | The teacher supervised the students during the exam. |
| 13 | **sit in** | Phrasal verb | Бути присутнім (як спостерігач) | To attend a class or meeting as an observer | Can I sit in on your lesson today? |
