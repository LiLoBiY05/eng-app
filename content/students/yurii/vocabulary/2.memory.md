# Memory & Brain Vocabulary

Words, idioms, and phrases related to memory, the brain, and cognitive processes.

## Memory Verb Phrases

| # | Word/Phrase | Part of Speech | Ukrainian | Definition | Example |
|---|-------------|----------------|-----------|------------|---------|
| 1 | **jog your memory** | Idiom | Освіжити пам'ять | To help someone remember something | Maybe this photo will jog your memory about the holiday. |
| 2 | **lose your memory** | Verb phrase | Втратити пам'ять | To become unable to remember things | He lost his memory after the accident. |
| 3 | **have a clear memory of** | Verb phrase | Чітко пам'ятати щось | To remember something very well | I have a clear memory of my first day at school. |
| 4 | **have a distant memory of** | Verb phrase | Смутно пам'ятати | To have an unclear recollection of something from long ago | I have a distant memory of that holiday — I was only three. |
| 5 | **have a good memory for** | Verb phrase | Добре пам'ятати (щось конкретне) | To be able to remember a particular type of thing easily | She has a good memory for faces but not for names. |
| 6 | **have a memory like a sieve** | Idiom | Пам'ять як решето | To be very forgetful | I've forgotten again — I have a memory like a sieve! |
| 7 | **have a photographic memory** | Idiom | Мати фотографічну пам'ять | To be able to remember things in exact detail | He has a photographic memory and can recall every page he reads. |
| 8 | **have a terrible memory for** | Verb phrase | Погано пам'ятати (щось) | To be unable to remember a particular type of thing | I have a terrible memory for numbers. |
| 9 | **have a vague memory of** | Verb phrase | Мати розпливчасте спогади | To have an unclear or uncertain recollection | I have a vague memory of meeting him somewhere before. |
| 10 | **have a vivid memory of** | Verb phrase | Мати яскравий спогад | To remember something very clearly and in detail | She has a vivid memory of the day she got the news. |
| 11 | **improve your memory** | Verb phrase | Покращити пам'ять | To make your ability to remember things better | Regular exercise can help improve your memory. |
| 12 | **think back to** | Phrasal verb | Згадувати щось, озиратися на минуле | To remember something from the past | When I think back to my childhood, I remember playing in the park. |
| 13 | **escape you** | Verb phrase | Вилетіти з голови | When you cannot remember something | His name escapes me at the moment — I'll remember it later. |
| 14 | **draw a blank** | Idiom | Нічого не пригадати | To be unable to remember something | I tried to remember her phone number, but I drew a blank. |
| 15 | **ring a bell** | Idiom | Щось знайоме | To sound familiar | That name rings a bell, but I can't remember where I heard it. |
| 16 | **slip someone's mind** | Idiom | Вилетіти з голови, забути | To forget something unintentionally | I'm sorry I didn't call — it completely slipped my mind. |
| 17 | **bear in mind** | Verb phrase | Тримати в голові | To remember and consider something when making decisions | Bear in mind that the shop closes at 6 PM. |
